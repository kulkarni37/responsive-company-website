Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can you start on a project?

After an initial discovery call and signed agreement, most projects kick off within two weeks. For urgent needs, we can often begin within 5 business days.

Do you work with startups or only large enterprises?

Both. We have dedicated teams for scale-up startups and separate enterprise practices. Our pricing and engagement models are designed to fit each context appropriately.

What does your support look like after launch?

We offer tiered managed services agreements — from email support with 48-hour response windows to fully dedicated SRE teams with 99.9% uptime SLAs and 15-minute response times.

Is the initial consultation really free?

Yes, completely. We'll spend up to 90 minutes understanding your challenge and sharing our honest perspective on the best path forward — whether that involves NovaTech or not.
